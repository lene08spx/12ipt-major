The Real World

Projectile motion plays a part in any game that uses a ball. Being able to calculate the motion of a ball lets robots play basketball, but also allows robotic chefs to move items around quickly. Learning about the Mathematics is vital to sending a rocket to space, the trajectory a rocket will take is government by projectile motion and being able to calculate that allows a rocket to travel very far for a very low fuel cost.
